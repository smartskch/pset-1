Welcome to Smart Challenge

We’re a group of anonymous 12th graders from SKCH. And we thought of giving people this opportunity to hone their problem solving and logic skills in a fun way. A disciplined approach to problem solving is the need of the hour due to the many problems we face in everyday life. Apart from a disciplined approach, creativity is also involved in the solution of many problems in life. Often, flawed solutions teach us how to correct our procedure of solution and to overcome the frustration that follows failure. Solving these questions will also improve your self confidence levels. We have included problems of different types-each requiring different thinking so as to give you this experience. Hope you enjoy!

Problem Set - 1

Problem 1.1. Liar! Liar!

  • Read the statements below and answer.

  1. The number of false statements here is one.
  2. The number of true statements here is two.
  3. The number of false statements here is three.

  4. The number of true statements here is four.

  • Which of the above statements is true?

Problem 1.2. Counterfeit!

  • There is a heap of 16 identical Rs 1 coins. But one of them is fake and varies only in weight-it’s either lighter or heavier than usual. Given an empty beam balance (No standard weights, sorry kids) how will you find the fake coin? Use the least no. of weighing attempts. (Hint: Think of the worst case possible)

Problem 1.3. Magic Shoes!

  • Satish is ‘x’ metres away from a wall. Every step he takes he covers half the remaining distance between him and the wall. In how many steps does he reach the wall?
